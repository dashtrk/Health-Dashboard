Time in Heart Rate Zones

Apple Watch zones computed here via Karvonen (heart rate reserve): max HR 188 bpm (Tanaka formula, age 28), resting HR 54 bpm, from continuous heart rate during tracked workouts. Whoop zones are Whoop's own calculation, exported per workout. Where both devices have data for the same month, values are averaged rather than summed — Apple Watch and Whoop overlap for several months, and adding both together would double-count the same workouts. "Below Z1" is Apple-only, since Whoop doesn't report that bucket.
